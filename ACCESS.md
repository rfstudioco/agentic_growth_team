# Access Policy

The Claude Growth Team repository is **private** and distributed **invite-only**. This file describes the gate, who may read it, and what access grants you.

## The gate

This repo is hosted as a **private GitHub repository**. Read access is granted manually by the maintainers on a named-individual basis. There is no public fork, mirror, or signup page.

If you arrived here without an invite — stop. Contact the maintainer first.

## Who can request access

- Growth marketers, founders, and operators evaluating Claude skills for their own org
- Agencies interested in a commercial license
- Students / researchers studying agentic growth systems (write in the request)
- Collaborators invited by RFStudio for a specific project

## How to request

Email **growth@rfstudio.co** with subject line:

```
Claude Growth Team — access request
```

Include:

1. Your full name and role
2. Company / project name + website
3. What you plan to do with the material (one paragraph)
4. Who referred you (if anyone)
5. Whether you're requesting **read-only**, **personal-use**, or **commercial** access

Expect a reply within 3–5 business days.

## What access grants you

| Tier | What you get | What you can do |
|---|---|---|
| **Read-only** | GitHub read permission | View, clone for personal reference |
| **Personal-use** | Read + local install rights | Install agents into your own Claude workspace for your own work |
| **Commercial** | Personal-use + resale/client-use | Run agents for paying clients (requires signed addendum) |

## What access does NOT grant you

- The right to **republish** the repo (public, forked, archive.org, gist, etc.)
- The right to **train models** on its contents
- The right to **repackage** agents as your own product
- The right to **share login / invite** with anyone else

Violations result in immediate revocation and, for commercial tiers, invoicing for unpaid usage at the maintainer's standard rate.

## Offboarding

Access is revoked automatically when:

- Your email bounces for 30+ days
- You leave the company under which access was granted (you can re-apply as an individual)
- The maintainer archives the repo (you retain previously-downloaded copies under LICENSE.md terms)
